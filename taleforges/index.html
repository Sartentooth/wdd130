What is Taleforges about?

Fighting alongside Luke Skywalker on the Death Star, destroying the One Ring with Frodo Baggins in the heart of Mordor, or fighting crime with Spider-Man. You always wanted to do it, but never found a place where you could make it happen? Don't worry about that I will make it happen. TaleForges is an online platform created so that you can live a unique experience in online role-playing games, you can not only create your adventures and direct them, but create vast worlds, captivating stories, unique characters, and all kinds of things that your imagination allows you. We give you all the tools, creators of maps and cities, dice of all kinds and even customize your own, databases with all kinds of weapons and armor, magic and spells, professions, names, vehicles, ships, and finally all kinds of elements that will make your experience something incredible.

How can I start in TaleForges?

You're probably wondering how much it would cost you to access TaleForges. Well, we have great news for you, taleForges fits everyone's economy, that's why it's freemium, and you can have access to all the tools of the platform. You can also access the single payment without a subscription and live a complete experience in your role-playing games. Being a gamified platform, you can share your achievements, progress, and campaigns with the community, receive trophies, and most importantly, enter the Hall Of Fame and achieve glory. The more you use, create, direct, and help others in the community, the more reputation you will achieve. And take your campaign and your players to the top. Can anyone play? Of course! That's the idea. For those who come to role-playing games for the first time, we will teach you how to use TaleForges with tutorials and you can also learn from the community.
