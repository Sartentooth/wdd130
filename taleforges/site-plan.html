Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to give you an entire platform where you can make your dreams come true, whatever game system you like such as Dragon & Dungeons, Lord of The Ring, Star Wars, and others, we offer you a space with plenty of tools and utilities for the design your world and unique campaigns and let the magic happen with your friends and fellows. Let us do the hard work and you only enjoy these experiences through our platform gamified, giving you a unique game environment

Audience

The site is fully oriented to Role-Playing games players and new ones who will discover a new world in immersive games with many hours of fun and fast-paced adventures as well as a place to make friends and forge together vibrant online stories.

Content

Home page

Fighting alongside Luke Skywalker on the Death Star, destroying the One Ring with Frodo Baggins in the heart of Mordor, or fighting crime with Spider-Man. You always wanted to do it, but never found a place where you could make it happen? Don't worry about that I will make it happen. TaleForges is an online platform created so that you can live a unique experience in online role-playing games, you can not only create your adventures and direct them, but create vast worlds, captivating stories, unique characters, and all kinds of things that your imagination allows you. We give you all the tools, creators of maps and cities, dice of all kinds and even customize your own, databases with all kinds of weapons and armor, magic and spells, professions, names, vehicles, ships, and finally all kinds of elements that will make your experience something incredible. You're probably wondering how much it would cost you to access TaleForges. Well, we have great news for you, taleForges fits everyone's economy, that's why it's freemium, and you can have access to all the tools of the platform. You can also access the single payment without a subscription and live a complete experience in your role-playing games. Being a gamified platform, you can share your achievements, progress, and campaigns with the community, receive trophies, and most importantly, enter the Hall Of Fame and achieve glory. The more you use, create, direct, and help others in the community, the more reputation you will achieve. And take your campaign and your players to the top. Can anyone play? Of course! That's the idea. For those who come to role-playing games for the first time, we will teach you how to use TaleForges with tutorials and you can also learn from the community.

System

The site is a platform for the development of RPG games of different types such as Dungeons & Dragons, Lotr, Pathfinder, Star Wars, etc. providing you with tools such as custom dice, thumbnails, character sheets, thematic customization of your account, maps and many third-party applications for an immersive experience. The objective of the activities is to give you a space where you can play role-playing games with your friends or with new players by publishing your campaign and you can not only have fun but also gain new skills, talents, and knowledge that will be useful not only here but in your daily life. Planning, organizing, creating, and managing a group of people and keeping the story of your campaigns always attractive will require all the experience you have and that you will earn in TaleForges. Not only are you going to have fun but you're going to learn new things. Both as a player and game master, you will have a complete glossary where you will be able to consult and learn how to use all the tools and applications that are available for your campaigns. There you will find how to use the menus, how to create your worlds, your characters, and maps, what elements you need, and what kind of dice and game systems. How to use the interface of the platform, what customization options you have, and the configuration of your account, etc. TaleForges is designed to retain users through playful activities typical of the human being. And we poured that life experience into the platform. We want you to have fun but at the same time grow and gain new skills in the best way than playing role-playing games, and this is not offered by any of the other similar sites.
